
# Case studies & future trends (quick briefing)

**Analytics types:** descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, prescriptive (+ real‑time).  
**Clinical:** sepsis risk monitoring; oncology precision medicine.  
**Population & SDOH:** risk tiers for proactive outreach; equity monitoring.  
**Operations & revenue:** ED flow forecasting; fraud & coding analytics.  
**Remote monitoring:** wearables, telehealth, and triage automation.  
**R&D:** multi‑modal data (clinical, imaging, genomics).

**Emerging:** gen‑AI assistants, real‑world evidence at scale, federated learning, and automation for coding/denials.  
**Challenges:** interoperability, quality/bias, security/compliance, change management.

*(This portfolio uses anonymized, synthetic‑style data to demonstrate methods only.)*
